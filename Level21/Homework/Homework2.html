About Flying Fish

Flying fish are unique creatures that can glide above the water using their specially adapted fins. They are found in warm waters around the world, and they can leap out of the water to escape predators or travel long distances.

Flying Fish Species

  • Golden Flying Fish - Known for their bright golden scales.
  • Blackwing Flying Fish - Characterized by dark, wide fins.
  • Silver-scaled Flying Fish - Often seen in schools near the surface of the ocean.
