Life of Pi

Story Summary

"Life of Pi" is a novel written by Yann Martel, which tells the remarkable story of Pi Patel, a young boy who survives a shipwreck and is stranded on a lifeboat in the Pacific Ocean with a Bengal tiger named Richard Parker. The novel explores themes of survival, spirituality, and the struggle between faith and reason.

The story is an extraordinary tale of perseverance, and Pi's faith in God keeps him going as he faces the challenges of isolation, hunger, and danger at sea.
