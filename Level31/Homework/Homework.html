Clint Eastwood

American actor, director, producer, and composer.

Biography

Clint Eastwood was born on May 31, 1930, in San Francisco, California. He is a legendary figure in Hollywood, known for his iconic roles in films such as "Dirty Harry", "The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly", and for his career as a director of films such as "Unforgiven", "Million Dollar Baby", and "Gran Torino".

Notable Movies

  • Dirty Harry (1971)
  • The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly (1966)
  • Unforgiven (1992)
  • Million Dollar Baby (2004)
  • Gran Torino (2008)
