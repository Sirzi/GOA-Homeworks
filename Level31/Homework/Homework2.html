About Joel Coen

Joel Coen is an American filmmaker, screenwriter, and producer. He is best known for co-directing films with his brother Ethan Coen.

Notable Films

  • Fargo (1996)
  • The Big Lebowski (1998)
  • No Country for Old Men (2007)
  • True Grit (2010)
