Bruce Lee: The Dragon Lives On

Biography Philosophy Legacy

Biography

Bruce Lee, also known as the Dragon, was a martial artist, actor, and philosopher. Born in San Francisco in 1940, he became a global icon and revolutionized martial arts in the 20th century.

Philosophy

Bruce Lee's philosophy of "Be Water, My Friend" emphasizes adaptability, flow, and resilience. His teachings transcend martial arts, inspiring millions worldwide to embrace self-expression and personal growth.

Legacy

Bruce Lee's legacy endures through his movies, books, and the countless people influenced by his way of life. He remains a symbol of strength, discipline, and cultural unity.
