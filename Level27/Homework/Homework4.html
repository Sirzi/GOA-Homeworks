Biography

Jet Li, born Li Lianjie, is a world-renowned martial artist, actor, and philanthropist from China. He rose to fame with his extraordinary skills in Wushu and has inspired millions with his martial arts and acting talent. Born in Beijing on April 26, 1963, he began training in Wushu at a very young age and won numerous national championships.

Famous Movies

Jet Li starred in many iconic movies that showcase his martial arts mastery. Some of his most famous works include:

  • Shaolin Temple (1982)
  • Once Upon a Time in China series (1991-1997)
  • Fist of Legend (1994)
  • Hero (2002)
  • Fearless (2006)

Legacy

Jet Li's impact on martial arts cinema is unparalleled. He has bridged cultures through his films and continues to contribute to humanitarian causes. As a global icon, his work has inspired generations of martial artists and movie enthusiasts alike.
