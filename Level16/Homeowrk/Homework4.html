About Apple

Apple Inc. is a global leader in consumer electronics, software, and services. Founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976, the company has revolutionized technology with products like the iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Apple is known for its emphasis on design, quality, and innovation.
