About Guinea Pigs

Guinea pigs, also known as cavies, are gentle, social creatures that make great pets. Originating from the Andes in South America, they are known for their distinct sounds and adorable looks.

Care Tips for Guinea Pigs

  • Provide a spacious cage with a comfortable bedding.
  • Ensure a balanced diet with fresh vegetables and hay.
  • Keep their cage clean and change bedding regularly.
  • Give them social time and play with them daily.
  • Always provide fresh water and keep them hydrated.
